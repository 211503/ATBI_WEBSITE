About ATBI

Pioneering Innovation & Excellence in Engineering Education

🎓

Who We Are

Andhra Technology Business Incubator (ATBI) is a premier innovation hub at Narasaraopeta Engineering College, dedicated to transforming ideas into successful ventures. We bridge the gap between academic excellence and entrepreneurial success, fostering a culture of innovation that drives technological advancement and economic growth.

🎯

Our Mission

To nurture and develop innovative minds by providing world-class incubation facilities, mentorship, and resources that transform cutting-edge ideas into sustainable businesses that contribute to societal and economic development.

🔮

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading technology business incubator that creates a thriving ecosystem of entrepreneurs, fostering innovation and excellence in engineering education while contributing to India's startup revolution.

🏆

Recognition & Accreditation

Ministry of MSME Recognition

Officially recognized by the Government of India's Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises for our outstanding contribution to entrepreneurship development and innovation ecosystem building across the region.

APSSDC Centre of Excellence

Designated as a premier Center of Excellence by the Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation, acknowledging our exceptional skill development programs and industry-aligned training initiatives.

International Collaborations

Strategic partnerships with prestigious global institutions including Stanford University, fostering international research collaborations and academic exchange programs.
